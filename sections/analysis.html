Real analysis prereq
Undergraduate real analysis on $\mathbb{R}$ and $\mathbb{R}^n$: completeness, $\varepsilon$–$\delta$ continuity, differentiation, uniform convergence, Riemann integration, multivariable diff/int, and the inverse function theorem.
ε–δ · MVT · Jacobian
Measure theory
Lebesgue measure via the Cantor set, simple functions, monotone & dominated convergence, $L^p$ spaces, and why Riemann fails where Lebesgue succeeds.
μ · ∫ · Lᵖ · DCT
Complex analysis prereq
Needham-style: holomorphic = amplitwist, Möbius transformations, domain coloring, contour integrals, residues, and the argument principle.
amplitwist · residues · Möbius
Functional analysis
Banach & Hilbert spaces, bounded operators, the four pillars (Hahn–Banach, open mapping, closed graph, uniform boundedness), spectrum, and the spectral theorem.

Operator algebras advanced
C*-algebras and Gelfand duality: a compact Hausdorff space and its commutative C*-algebra of functions are the same data. Functional calculus, characters, the Gelfand transform, and the noncommutative turn.
A ↔ Spec(A)
Dynamical systems advanced
Orbits, phase portraits, bifurcations, chaos, Lyapunov exponents, and strange attractors — continuous and discrete dynamics you can drag, perturb, and watch evolve.
orbits · bifurcation · chaos
Harmonic analysis & Fourier theory

Plancherel · convolution · uncertainty · Poisson
Sobolev spaces & distributions
Test functions, $\mathcal D'$, weak derivatives, $W^{k,p}$ and the embedding zoo, trace theorems, fundamental solutions, and the variational form of $-\Delta u = f$.
distributions · $W^{k,p}$ · embeddings · Lax–Milgram
Partial differential equations
Classification (elliptic / parabolic / hyperbolic), heat / wave / Laplace equations, weak solutions and Sobolev regularity.
heat · wave · weak

Laplace · Poisson · Harnack

Operator spectra, self-adjoint and compact operators, the spectral theorem in compact and bounded forms, unbounded operators.
spectrum · self-adjoint · PVM
